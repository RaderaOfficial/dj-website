HARDEST ACT IN SWEDEN

As she always says, “My heart belongs to Hardcore.”

From the underground to the world’s biggest stages, Hysta has taken the Hardcore scene by storm with her infinite dedication and signature melodic sound. Born in France & raised across continents on a sailing boat, her unique past shaped her dream: “Make Hardcore Worldwide.” Thanks to her endless creativity and drive, she is pushing hardcore’s boundaries from Asia and Oceania to North and South America, the Middle East, and every corner of Europe. In record time, Hysta has claimed her spot in the world’s biggest Dance events, including Tomorrowland, Defqon.1, Masters of Hardcore, Dominator, Thunderdome, Mysteryland, and Dreamfields Mexico.

With releases on Masters of Hardcore and Q-dance Music, she has collaborated with icons like Korsakoff, F. Noize, and Re-Style. Her tracks, including ‘Always Rise’ (#7 MOH TOP 100 in 2024), ‘Choose Life’ (#7 MOH TOP 100 in 2023), ‘Gabber Madness’, ‘Make You Bounce’, and ‘Broken Dreams’, have amassed dozens of millions of streams, cementing her as one of the genre’s most promising new forces. Above this, in 2024, she was responsible for the Masters of Hardcore Austria Anthem.

Showing no signs of slowing down, she takes the experience even further with her LIVE ACT: “Dance with the Wolves,” adorned in striking wolf warrior-inspired attire, an exclusive show featuring exclusive music and stunning visuals.

For Hysta, success isn’t just about rising to the top; it’s about keeping Hardcore alive and making it stronger than ever. Backed by her ever-growing and devoted fanbase, she is now building her legacy with her own event concept, “Welcome To Wolf City,” a fully immersive club tour and stage hosting experience, designed to unite the community, elevate new talent, and celebrate Hardcore in its purest form.
